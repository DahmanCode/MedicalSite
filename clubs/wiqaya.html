program wiqaya

description :

Program WiQaya, this first edition on may 2022 it targets young people whose admitted to public health workshops. The goal of the program is to spread awareness and sensitize in the first place through a group of workshops as follows :

  1. first aid
  2. pre-baccalaureate orientation and enhancement of nursing sciences
  3. adolescent reproductive health
  4. addictology and the healthy diet
